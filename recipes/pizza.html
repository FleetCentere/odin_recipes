Pizza

This is a recipe to make pizza!

Ingredients for Pizza

  • Pizza Dough
  • Pasta
  • Cheese
  • Butter
  • Sauce
  • A pan
  • An oven
  • Hard work

Steps to make pizza

  1. Spread the bread out on the pan
  2. Bake the bread for 5-7 minutes
  3. Take out the pan with the dough
  4. Add the sauce, cheese, and other related topings
  5. Finish cooking the pizza
  6. Take out the finsihed pizza and let it cool for a few minutes
